Outdoor activities

Norway is a natural playground!

Rock Climbing

Challenge yourself on Norway’s famous cliffs! It offers amazing climbing routes with unbeatable views.

Kayaking

Paddle through calm, glittering waters. It is the best way to get up close to Norway's stunning fjords.

Mountain Hiking

Put on your hiking boots! Norway offers the most breathtaking mountain trails and viewpoints in the world.

Ride a friendly Norwegian Fjord horse! Explore historic trails and deep valleys just like ancient vikings.
